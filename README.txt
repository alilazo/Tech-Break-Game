In oreder to start the game use Imaginary_Battlefield.exe
Use numpad in order to use the menu.

When the game starts you will see your battle points and your health. Spend your battle points on your hand
and end your turn when you can not do so anymore. It will then be the second players turn, and they will do the same.
At the end of very round you will be able to give yourself an extra Battle point into stamina or mana. 
You can have a max of 10 points in each for a total of 20 battle points. First player to have no HP losses.
